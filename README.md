# HELM — Personal Automation Platform

> **Your own AI that runs 24/7 on your machine. Not a subscription to a service. Yours.**

---

## Get started

**→ [get-helm.github.io](https://get-helm.github.io)**

The setup guide walks you through everything — no terminal, no technical background needed. One click copies a prompt; an AI walks you through the rest.

---

## What changes after you set this up

- You wake up to a summary of your calendar, emails that need replies, and what changed overnight
- You describe a task in plain English and get a finished result — not a starting point
- The repetitive things you do every week start happening automatically

---

## What you need

- A **Claude subscription** — the AI that powers HELM (~$20/month at [claude.ai](https://claude.ai))
- A **computer you can leave on 24/7** — a Mac Mini works great, or any Windows PC you're not using daily
- About **45–60 minutes** to set up

---

## How it works

HELM lives in a Discord server you create. You talk to it like a person. It manages workspaces, runs automations on schedules, and surfaces things you need to know — without you having to ask.

The guided setup creates your Discord server, connects the bot, and walks you through your first preferences. Everything else happens over time.
